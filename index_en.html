A SCOOP THE FIRST ELECTRIC BATTERY ROCK HARD FOR THE WORLD

The first Scoop Batteries for Hard Rock in the World, developed entirely by GE and introduced in Peru by AESA Infrastructure and Construction, will start operating from next month in Peru.

This is a truly revolutionary development for underground mining, for the many benefits it brings, operational, economic and health levels.

  • We expect to obtain significant savings in RAISE BORING and ventilation.

  • Environmental protection and health of workers, since an internal combustion engine is replaced by an electric motor

  • The e-scoop has a radius of unlimited action unlike a conventional electric scoop carrying a power cord which limits. This advantage allows it to operate more efficiently.
